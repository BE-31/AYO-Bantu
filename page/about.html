why are we formed?

The world is currently facing a speed in creating a trans-nationalistic society where people have merged into one big society due to digitalization and globalization to address current world problems such as pandemics, global warming, and climate change.

These problems are also faced by friends with disabilities. Solving this problem will reduce our time and effort that could be used in marketing, engagement and providing more support to people with disabilities while allowing us to reach more individuals who will benefit from us.
